About World Surf League


The World Surf League helps to promote and set up the annual tour of professional surf competitions. Each event is broadcasted live at WorldSurfLeague.com where you can feel like you’re part of the action at anytime from anyplace it’s on. Be it live event broadcasts, social updates, event highlights and commentary either on desktop or mobile, allows a viewer to fully immerse themselves in the events.

The professional surf tour was founded in 1976. It was founded on the primes of recognizing, elite athletes, diverse fans and dedicated partners that embody professional surfing today. Offices are found throughout the globe, with headquarters based in Los Angeles, California.

The World Surf League is dedicated to:
*Bringing the athleticism, drama and adventure of pro surfing to fans worldwide.

*Promoting professional surfers as world-class athletes.

*Acting as good stewards of the environment both in and out of the water.

The WSL exists to celebrate and grow the history, elite athletes, diverse fans and dedicated partners that together embody professional surfing today.
